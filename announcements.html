New Highway Project Launched

October 1, 2025 | General News

The Ministry of Transport has officially launched a new major highway project aimed at connecting key cities and improving national infrastructure. This project is expected to significantly boost economic activity and facilitate easier travel across the country.

The highway will span over 300 kilometers, connecting Damascus with major economic centers. Construction is expected to be completed within 3 years, creating thousands of jobs and improving logistics efficiency across the region.

Key Features: Four-lane design, modern toll systems, rest areas every 50km, and integration with existing road networks.

Public Transport Expansion Initiative

September 28, 2025 | Services

An ambitious initiative to expand public transport networks in major urban centers has been announced. This includes new bus routes, increased frequency, and modernizing existing fleets to provide better service to commuters.

The expansion will add 50 new buses to the fleet, introduce 15 new routes, and implement a digital ticketing system. The initiative aims to reduce traffic congestion and provide affordable transportation options to all citizens.

New Regulations for Commercial Vehicles

September 20, 2025 | Regulations

The Ministry has introduced new regulations for commercial vehicles to enhance road safety and ensure compliance with international standards. These regulations cover vehicle maintenance, driver training, and cargo security.

All commercial vehicle operators must comply with the new standards by January 2026. The regulations include mandatory safety inspections every 6 months, driver certification programs, and updated cargo securing requirements.

International Aviation Safety Standards Adopted

September 15, 2025 | Aviation

Syria's aviation sector is aligning with the latest international safety standards as part of an ongoing commitment to ensuring secure and efficient air travel. This move will enhance global connectivity and passenger confidence.

The adoption of ICAO standards demonstrates Syria's commitment to aviation safety and will facilitate better integration with international air traffic systems.

Port Modernization Project Underway

September 10, 2025 | Maritime

Major modernization efforts are in progress at key Syrian ports to improve cargo handling efficiency, increase capacity, and enhance trade logistics. This project is vital for regional and international commerce.

The modernization includes new container handling equipment, expanded storage facilities, and digital port management systems. The project will increase port capacity by 40% and reduce ship turnaround time by 30%.

New Driving License System Implemented

September 5, 2025 | Services

A new, digitized driving license issuance and renewal system has been implemented to streamline processes and reduce wait times. Citizens can now apply and track their applications online.

The digital system includes online appointment booking, document upload capabilities, and real-time application tracking. Processing times have been reduced from 2 weeks to 3-5 business days.

Road Safety Awareness Campaign Launched

August 28, 2025 | Safety

A nationwide road safety awareness campaign has been launched to educate drivers and pedestrians on safe practices, aiming to reduce traffic accidents and promote responsible road use.

The campaign includes television advertisements, school programs, and community workshops. Statistics show that proper road safety education can reduce accidents by up to 25%.

New Logistics Hub Inaugurated

August 20, 2025 | Infrastructure

A state-of-the-art logistics hub has been inaugurated to enhance freight transportation and supply chain efficiency across the region. This facility will serve as a central point for distribution and storage.

The 50,000 square meter facility includes temperature-controlled storage, modern loading docks, and direct highway access. It will handle up to 1,000 trucks daily and create 500 new jobs.

Call for Proposals: Green Transport Initiatives

August 10, 2025 | Events

The Ministry is inviting proposals for innovative green transport initiatives to promote sustainable practices and reduce carbon emissions in the transportation sector. Funding opportunities are available.

Proposals are being accepted for projects involving electric vehicles, alternative fuels, public transport improvements, and cycling infrastructure. Funding grants range from $50,000 to $500,000. Deadline for submissions is November 30, 2025.
