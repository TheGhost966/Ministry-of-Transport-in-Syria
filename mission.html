Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles for a sustainable and efficient transportation future

Our Mission

To develop, regulate, and maintain a safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation system that supports economic growth, enhances national connectivity, and improves the quality of life for all citizens of Syria. We are committed to fostering innovation, ensuring accessibility, and upholding the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility across all modes of transport.

Key Pillars of Our Mission:

  • Safety: Prioritizing the safety and security of passengers and cargo.
  • Efficiency: Optimizing transport networks for speed and reliability.
  • Sustainability: Promoting environmentally friendly transport solutions.
  • Connectivity: Enhancing national and international links.
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and practices.

Our Vision

To be a leading regional ministry recognized for its advanced, integrated, and resilient transportation infrastructure that serves as a cornerstone for Syria's prosperity. We envision a future where all citizens have access to modern, affordable, and seamless transport options, contributing to a vibrant economy and a connected society.

Our Vision for the Future:

  • Integrated Networks: Seamless connections across all transport modes.
  • Technological Advancement: Utilizing smart technologies for transport management.
  • Environmental Leadership: Pioneering green transport initiatives.
  • Public Satisfaction: Meeting and exceeding the expectations of our users.
  • Regional Hub: Positioning Syria as a key transport hub in the region.

Our Core Values

These values guide our actions and decisions every day.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our operations and interactions.

Service Excellence

Committing to provide high-quality, responsive, and citizen-focused services.

Innovation

Continuously seeking new and better ways to improve transportation and operational efficiency.

Environmental Stewardship

Protecting the environment through sustainable practices and green transport initiatives.

Safety

Ensuring the highest levels of safety and security for all transportation modes and users.

Accountability

Taking responsibility for our actions and being transparent in our decision-making processes.
