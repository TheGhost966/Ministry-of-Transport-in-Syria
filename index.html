New Highway Project Launched

October 1, 2025

The Ministry of Transport is pleased to announce the launch of a major new highway project that will connect Damascus with Aleppo through a modern, multi-lane expressway. This project represents a significant investment in Syria's infrastructure and will reduce travel time between the two cities by approximately 30%.

The highway will feature state-of-the-art safety systems, rest areas, and service facilities. Construction is expected to begin in early 2026, with completion targeted for 2028. This project will create thousands of jobs and significantly improve transportation efficiency across the country.

For more information, please contact our public relations department or visit our office.

Public Transport Expansion

September 28, 2025

The Ministry of Transport has expanded public bus services with new routes serving previously underserved neighborhoods across major cities. This expansion is part of our commitment to providing accessible and affordable public transportation for all citizens.

The new routes include connections to residential areas, commercial districts, and educational institutions. Modern buses equipped with air conditioning and accessibility features will serve these routes. The expansion will benefit over 50,000 daily commuters.

Railway Modernization Program

September 25, 2025

A comprehensive railway modernization program has been initiated to upgrade tracks, stations, and rolling stock. This multi-year investment will significantly improve passenger comfort, safety, and journey times across the national rail network.

Safety Campaign Launched

September 20, 2025

The Ministry has launched a nationwide road safety awareness campaign focusing on reducing traffic accidents and promoting safe driving practices. The campaign includes educational programs in schools, media outreach, and enhanced enforcement.

Digital Services Portal

September 15, 2025

Citizens can now access transport-related services online through our new digital portal. Services include license renewals, vehicle registration, appointment booking, and document requests. The portal is available 24/7 for your convenience.
