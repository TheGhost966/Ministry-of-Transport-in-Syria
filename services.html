Our Services

Comprehensive transportation services for all citizens

Driving License Services

We provide comprehensive driving license services including new applications, renewals, and replacements. Our modern testing facilities ensure fair and efficient licensing procedures.

Services Include:

  • New driving license applications
  • License renewals and updates
  • International driving permits
  • Driving tests and examinations
  • License replacement for lost/damaged cards

Processing Time: 3-5 business days

Required Documents: ID card, medical certificate, photos

Apply Now

Vehicle Registration

Register your vehicle quickly and efficiently through our streamlined registration process. We handle all types of vehicles from private cars to commercial trucks.

Services Include:

  • New vehicle registration
  • Vehicle ownership transfer
  • Registration renewal
  • License plate issuance
  • Vehicle inspection certificates

Processing Time: 1-3 business days

Required Documents: Purchase invoice, ID, insurance

Apply Now

Transport Permits

Obtain the necessary permits for commercial transport operations, special vehicle movements, and cross-border transportation activities.

Services Include:

  • Commercial transport licenses
  • Special vehicle movement permits
  • Cross-border transport permits
  • Oversize load permits
  • Taxi and bus operator licenses

Processing Time: 5-7 business days

Required Documents: Business license, vehicle docs, insurance

Apply Now

Road Safety Programs

Participate in our road safety education programs designed to promote safe driving practices and reduce traffic accidents across Syria.

Programs Include:

  • Defensive driving courses
  • School safety education
  • Corporate safety training
  • Public awareness campaigns
  • First aid and emergency response

Duration: Varies by program

Cost: Free for most programs

Learn More

Vehicle Inspection

Ensure your vehicle meets safety and environmental standards through our comprehensive inspection services available at authorized centers nationwide.

Services Include:

  • Annual safety inspections
  • Emissions testing
  • Pre-purchase inspections
  • Commercial vehicle inspections
  • Inspection certificate issuance

Frequency: Annual (required)

Locations: 25+ authorized centers
